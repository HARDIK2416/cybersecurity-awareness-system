Common Online Scams

Awareness is the first step to safety. Learn these common scams and warning signs.

📱

OTP Fraud

  • Never share OTP with anyone.
  • Banks never ask for OTP on calls.
  • Be careful with fake customer care numbers.
💼

Fake Job Offers

  • Be careful if they ask for registration fees.
  • Verify company email and website.
  • Avoid offers that sound too easy.
🛒

Fake Shopping Links

  • Check website spelling carefully.
  • Avoid unknown links from messages.
  • Use trusted payment methods.
🎁

Lottery and Prize Scam

  • Do not pay money to claim a prize.
  • Do not share bank details.
  • Fake urgency is a warning sign.
📈

Investment Scam

  • Be careful with guaranteed high returns.
  • Research before investing.
  • Do not trust random Telegram/WhatsApp tips.
👤

Fake Social Profile

  • Verify identity before trusting.
  • Do not send money to unknown people.
  • Protect your personal photos and data.

Safe Browsing Tips

Follow these habits daily to reduce cyber risk.

✅

Daily Safety Checklist

  • Use strong and unique passwords.
  • Enable 2FA on important accounts.
  • Update phone, apps, and browser.
  • Do not click unknown links.
  • Download apps only from trusted stores.
  • Check website URL before login.
🧩

Smart Internet Habits

  • Think before sharing personal details.
  • Do not trust messages creating panic.
  • Verify offers from official websites.
  • Use screen lock on phone and laptop.
  • Logout from shared computers.
  • Backup important files regularly.

Report Cybercrime Guide

If you face cyber fraud, do not panic. Save screenshots, transaction details, phone numbers, email IDs, links, and chat proof. Do not delete evidence. Report through the official cybercrime portal or contact local cyber police.
